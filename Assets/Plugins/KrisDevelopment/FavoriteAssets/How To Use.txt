Developed by: Hristo Ivanov
Asset Store Publisher: Kris Development

HOW TO USE:
Navigate to "Kris Development > Favorite Assets".
Dock the window somewhere you see fit.
Click on an asset and press "Pin Selected Assets" to add an asset to the list.
Press an asset from the list to instantly locate it it in the editor
or press "Open" to instantly open the asset with the according software;
"X" will remove the asset from the pinned assets list.
